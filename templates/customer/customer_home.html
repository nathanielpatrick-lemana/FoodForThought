Welcome to Frankie's Italian Cuisine!

At Frankie's, we have a history dating back over 100 years of culinary excellence. Founded in 1903 in Derry, New Hampshire by first-generation immigrant Frank Fillocini, we have become southern New Hampshire's best Italian establishment. We hope we deliver the phenomenal dining experience we strive to give you!

Find us at 71 North Main Street, Derry, NH 03038. We are open every day except Monday, from 11:30am until 9:00pm (last order at 8:30pm). We are operating at 50 percent capacity due to COVID-19. We deliver and offer curbside pickup!
